faq

Can I order online and get my items shipped to me?

Absolutely! Simply add all the items that you wish to order from our website into your shopping cart and click on the shopping cart to check out. If you need are in need of any assistance, please feel free to contact us.

How do I track my order?

Once you finish placing your order, you will receive an email confirmation stating that your order has been received. This email will also include tracking information that you will need to be able to see where your package is.

Do you deliver outside of the US?

Yes, we deliver worldwide from our workshop in New York.

Do you test your products on animals?

Spoke(n) is proud to be a cruelty-free company. All of our products are certified as not tested on animals under the Cruelty Free International Leaping Bunny Programme (2018), which is the international gold standards for products that are free from animal testing.

shipping

options

When you place an order with us, you’ll be happy to know that it will be carefully packaged and shipped within 1 business day. You will receive the tracking information by email as soon as it leaves our workshop. We ship to anywhere in Canada and USA, as well as to many international destinations.

  • USA Shipping Rates

Standard ~$5.3 USD* (2 - 7 Business Days). Free for orders over ~$58 USD*

Xpresspost ~$11.25* (2 - 4 Business Days).

*USD shipping rates are clearly shown during checkout and calculated from CAD using live mid-market exchange rates.

  • Canadian Shipping Rates

Canada Post Expedited $6.95 (4 – 8 business days). Free for orders over $75

Canada Post Express $17.95 (3 – 4 business days).

  • International Shipping Rates

International shipping rates are calculated by weight during checkout.

International Air service (1 to 2 weeks - not tracked).

Tracked Package (1 to 2 weeks).

Please contact your customs authority to find out if any additional import fees or taxes will be charged.

Missing Item Policy

Although we check and double check orders prior to shipping, mistakes do happen. If an item is missing, please let us know. We can ship it out to you, issue a refund, or include it in your next order!
