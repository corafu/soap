our philisophy

We believe that it is possible to make small, yet effective changes to your lifestyle that benefit your overall health. We are committed to raising the bar and setting new standards for natural products. To us, the quality and the formulas of our natural products are important; therefore, we make these products without compromising on ingredients or user experience. Our natural soaps are made using the traditional cold process method and are all handmade in small batches to ensure we maintain our high quality. Our unique blends of natural oils and butters are selected for their skin-nurturing properties. All our skin care recipes are safety assessed by a qualified cosmetic chemist.

With a love for natural or organic products, we are passionate about our company, as well as spreading the word on the importance of natural and green beauty. At Spoke(n), we love what we do and will continue to formulate and bring you the best in our all-natural and plant-based skin care products.

our ingredients

We use the ingredients that are derived from nature such as botanicals and mineral-derived ingredients that are packed with healthy benefits for our bodies. Everything we produce is made without harming any animals. We never buy ingredients from suppliers that test their products on animals. Spoke(n) is committed to staying certified vegan and cruelty-free. Our products do not contain synthetics, chemicals, preservatives or anything artificial.

Our natural soap bars are made primarily from the following ingredients:

  • Salts
  • Coconut Oil
  • Avocado Oil
  • Olive Oil
  • Castor Oil
  • Essential Oils
  • Shea Butter
  • Clays and Botanicals

We find that these oils represent the best combination of nutrients and vitamins that keep your skin healthy and soft. Our soaps also contain essential oils that are gentle on your skin to create our custom scents. For people who are sensitive to aromas, we have soaps that are scent-free. Our soaps also contain natural exfoliants such as oatmeal, peppermint leaves, sea salt, and sand to provide your skin or face with a thorough but gentle scrub. The color of our products are derived solely from pure clays and botanicals - no chemical colorants, mica's or oxides. Every ingredient has a purpose that cares for your skin and nothing less.
